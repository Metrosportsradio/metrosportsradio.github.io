About Us

Hosted by Jamie in the "KINGdom", Metropolitan Sports Radio or MSR for short is all about baseball and basketball. Every week, our broadcasts will bring you insights and analysis of the hottest topics around the league. Make sure to tune in to our weekly Sunday broadcasts, and check our Facebook page for the dates of our weekly weekday broadcasts (times are subject to change). All broadcasts are recorded and uploaded to Youtube channel, "Metro Sports Radio". Making our broadcasts availiable to you anytime, anyday, anywhere. Make sure to subscribe to our Youtube channel, like and share our Facebook page. If you enjoy listening to MSR, make sure to spread the word to your friends, family, colleagues, peers, etc. Have a great day, a remember to tune in whenever you can!

You are more than welcome to call-in to the "KINGdom" with your questions or topics that would you like for us to talk about during our live sessions. All you need to do is to follow these simple intructions:

  • 1) Become friends with SCMKING on Skype
  • 2) Send a message before you call-in
  • 3) Wait for our reply, then you are set!
